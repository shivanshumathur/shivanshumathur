Project Vision


Hi Travelers, planning to go on a trip with multiple destinations and stops? Indian Railways provides the facility of booking Circular Journey Tickets. These tickets offer you unique travel flexibility, as they are issued for all journeys (other than regular routes), which begin and complete at the same station.

Odyssey is great to use for planning circular journey & quick train tickets booking, but it's also useful for route selection, break selection, train selection and train availability. By designing a both compact and accessible interface, I feel that anyone can use it easily.

Rules

1. A maximum of 8 break is permitted in the complete journey.
2. Journey must Start and End at same point.
3. Journey Distance = Sum of Journey Days (400 km = 1 Day) and Break Journey Days (200 km = 1 Day)
4. Cost of circular journey depends on 'Class of Travel', 'Total Distance', 'Number of Seats', 'Senior Concession'.
